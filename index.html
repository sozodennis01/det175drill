AFROTC Drill Simulator

Time: 3:00

Use arrow keys to move. Press F to interact with the evaluator (blue square) and answer prompts. Press SPACEBAR to turn around (about face). After speaking with the evaluator, move to the green circle to take your position. Complete the drill within the 3-minute time limit and avoid hitting boundaries.

Available Commands:

1 Fall In
2 Attention
3 Left Face
4 Right Face
5 About Face
6 Parade Rest
Z Halt
W Forward March
A Column Left
D Column Right
Q Left Flank
E Right Flank
S To The Rear March

Note: Commands will be visible in the top-right of the screen. You must issue the FALL IN command first before using other commands.
